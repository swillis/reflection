Features

We're launching early next year with detailed revenue, rank and download data for apps in the iPhone and iPad stores.

Global App Leaderboard

View revenue and download data for the Top 400 apps in any country, in any category, at any time.

Revenue & Download Data

Analyse historical revenue, ranking and download information for any app to base your business decisions on.

Beautiful & Functional

Access meaningful market intelligence wherever you are on any device through our easy to use web app.

Supported Stores

We will launch with the iPhone and iPad stores and will then work hard to add all major stores starting with Google Play.

Comprehensive Search

Search for any app to see its revenue, download and ranking history for any country and date range.

Powerful Statistical Model

We have created an algorithm that statistically paints a very accurate picture of the stores based on public ranking and private download and revenue data.

Make Smarter Decisions

Analyze daily revenue, downloads and ranking for the Top 400 apps in any country/category. Make smart business, marketing and development decisions by comparing the performance of similar apps.
